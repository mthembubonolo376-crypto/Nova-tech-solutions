FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Find answers to common questions about bookings, payments, and account management.

How do I book a service?

Visit the Book Service page, fill out the form with your organisation details, service type, preferred date, and location. Submit and you'll receive a booking ID and invoice reference immediately.

How quickly can you respond to my booking?

Most bookings are confirmed within 24 hours. Emergency requests can be escalated for same-day response. Contact our support team for urgent needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept online payments via PayFast and Stripe (credit/debit cards). Schools and NGOs can also use invoice-based billing with typical net 30 payment terms.

Can multiple staff use our organisation account?

Yes! Our platform supports multi-user organisation accounts. Multiple staff can submit bookings, view history, and manage invoices from one centralised account.

How do I track my service request?

Log into your Client Portal to view all your active bookings, their status (pending, confirmed, assigned, in-progress, completed), and invoice details.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

Contact our support team via the Contact page. Cancellations made 48 hours before the scheduled time are free. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

How is my data stored and secured?

All booking and contact information is stored securely in your browser's local storage. We follow POPIA compliance guidelines for South African organisations. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Do you offer recurring/maintenance contracts?

Yes! For ongoing maintenance at schools and offices, we offer monthly or quarterly service agreements with consolidated billing.

Can I get an invoice for my records?

Absolutely. Each service automatically generates a detailed invoice with a unique invoice number for your accounting system.
