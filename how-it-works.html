How It Works

The complete service booking workflow.

From booking to completion, here's how Nova Tech Support manages your service requests.

The booking journey

1. Submit Request

Your organisation fills out the booking form with service type, location, preferred date, and any special requirements.

2. Confirmation

Nova Tech Support receives your request and immediately confirms receipt. An invoice ID is generated for billing.

3. Technician Assignment

Our operations team assigns the most suitable technician based on location, availability, and expertise.

4. Service Delivery

The technician arrives on schedule and completes the work. You can track status in real-time.

5. Completion & Invoice

Work is marked complete, and an invoice is generated for your records. Payment can be submitted online or via invoice workflow.

6. Support Follow-up

We maintain a full service history for your organisation. Future requests reference past work for continuity.

Booking status tracking

Pending

Your request has been received and is awaiting confirmation and technician assignment.

Confirmed

Nova Tech has confirmed the booking and is preparing to assign a technician.

Assigned

A technician has been assigned to your request. You'll receive contact details and an ETA.

In Progress

The technician is on-site and actively working on your service request.

Completed

Work is complete. An invoice has been generated and is available in your portal.

For organisations

Multi-user access

Multiple staff members at your organisation can submit bookings, view status, and manage payments from one account.

Centralised reporting

View all service history, invoices, and performance metrics in one dashboard.

Payment flexibility

Choose online payment or invoice-based billing that fits your organisation's accounting workflow.
